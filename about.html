My Journey in Climate Action

About Me

Though I'm just beginning my work, I'm dedicated to raising awareness about climate change in Uganda and beyond. My focus is on empowering communities and advocating for sustainable practices that can make a real difference in people's lives.

As a youth climate advocate, I believe in the power of grassroots movements and community engagement. My journey started with a simple realization: climate change affects us all, but its impacts are felt most severely in vulnerable communities like those in Uganda.
